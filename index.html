FAQ

Any Questions? Answered

Explore our frequently asked questions to find answers to common inquiries about our services, fees, investment options, and more.

To determine the minimum investment required, please refer to the pricing details in the navigation pane or scroll up to access our comprehensive range of minimum pricing packages available at Greystone. Our investment options cater to various needs, ensuring accessibility and flexibility for our clients.
At Greystone, profit distribution is intricately tied to our equity share equations. We operate on a model where you can deposit or withdraw funds from the investment pool. Should you seek to withdraw profits, they are disbursed accordingly, with a proportional adjustment in your fund share. This method ensures a transparent and equitable approach to profit management.
While we pride ourselves on offering industry-leading investment strategies, customization options are limited as we rely on sophisticated automated trading and risk management systems at Greystone. Our emphasis is on leveraging proven methodologies and cutting-edge technology to optimize returns while managing risk effectively for all our clients.
Our trading strategies are a blend of technical analysis, artificial intelligence, and data-driven insights. While we cannot divulge specific proprietary details, our approach encompasses a spectrum of methodologies such as statistical modeling, LSTM and GRU networks, and strategic alignment with economic data. This comprehensive strategy framework enables us to navigate dynamic market conditions with agility and precision.
Returns at Greystone are realized through a buy-in and sell-out policy rather than a structured distribution schedule. This approach ensures that returns are aligned with market movements and investment performance, providing a fluid and responsive mechanism for optimizing returns based on prevailing market conditions.
Our fee structure is designed to align with your investment journey seamlessly. Fees are structured based on your investment amount and are billed monthly, reflecting a transparent and accountable approach to financial management. These fees are only realized upon account withdrawal, ensuring that they are directly tied to the performance and benefits you receive from our services.
Risk management is a cornerstone of our approach at Greystone, encompassing a diverse range of strategies tailored to mitigate potential market uncertainties. From employing tight stop-loss mechanisms to utilizing trailing stops, grid strategies, and hedging methods, our risk management systems are designed to safeguard your investments while optimizing opportunities for growth.
Our lock-up period is structured in correlation with your investment size, denoted in dollar terms. Detailed information regarding lock-up periods can be found within our pricing sections, providing clarity and transparency regarding investment timelines and commitments.
